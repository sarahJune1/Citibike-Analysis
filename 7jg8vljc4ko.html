Methodology -
We were suggested by Mengyun Li in our peer review to use a z-test would have been appropriate if we were comparing one sample to its population.  However, we compare two different samples (customers vs. subscribers). 
Our other suggestion from our peer reviewer, Tanya Nabila, suggested a Pearson's test.  Yet, we don't believe it is sufficient for testing our hypothesis, because we are not looking for a linear relationship between the two samples. Instead, we will implement a t-test
