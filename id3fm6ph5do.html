The bike share initiative of New York City is blue Citi Bikes, docked throughout the streets. People can purchase an annual subscription or a shorter day pass. Ideal for one-way trips, joy-rides through Central Park, or the daily commute. We were curious to see how the bike share program is used between the different user types. Each pass type has its unique time parameters on how long the bike can be used
